Later I've worked for 2 years with BI developers, creating reports, jobs and ETLs using Pentaho, and also designed and administrated our PostgreSQL database. Ultimately, I've been working as a full-stack developer, using Java, AngularJS and Bootstrap.


I've been learning Swift for iOS development and Ruby on Rails on my spare time. Always trying to keep myself updated :) I'm passionate about clean and smart code, which I believe is more than just an engineering work, it's a form of art.
